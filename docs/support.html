Mognator Support Page

FAQ

Q1: What is Mognator?

A: Mognator is an app that suggests optimal restaurants based on your current mood using an AI-powered question-based recommendation algorithm.

Q2: Why is location access required?

A: Location is needed to search for nearby restaurants and suggest venues within your reach. Location data is not stored on servers and is only used temporarily for searches.

Q3: Where is my data stored?

A: All learning data (question answer history, etc.) is stored locally on your device. It is not transmitted to external servers.

Q4: Can I delete my learning data?

A: Yes. Go to Settings â†’ "Reset Learning Data" to delete your data anytime.

Q5: Where does restaurant information come from?

A: We use Google Places API (New) to retrieve the latest restaurant information.

Q6: What are scoring settings?

A: A feature to customize how restaurants are ranked. Adjust weights for "rating", "review count", "open now", "distance", and "price level" to match your priorities.

Q7: Does it work offline?

A: No. Internet connection is required for restaurant searches.

Q8: Is there a fee?

A: Mognator is currently completely free to use.
